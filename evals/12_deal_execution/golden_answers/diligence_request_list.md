## Notes

- 72 specific items is toward the lower end of the 80-120 range;
  this version trades breadth for specificity. A project-specific
  expansion in regulatory and reimbursement subcategories (e.g.,
  by state) would bring the count into range without adding
  genuinely new diligence categories.
- Healthcare-specific items are front-loaded: regulatory,
  reimbursement, and HR/staffing together account for 24 Critical
  requests (out of 43 total), reflecting where deal-break risk
  typically sits for home health and hospice.
- The list is a starting point, not a final one — confirmatory
  diligence will generate follow-ons based on initial responses.
